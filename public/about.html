The Faces of Our Company

Booflix was founded by three Btech students at the first year of thier bachelor's degree, namely by Ansh Pradhan, Venkata Narsimha Rao and Ashish Chaubey. They had a vision to create a website in which the users can watch all of thier favourite content for free. Thier vision became reality when one of the co-founders namely Ansh suggested that they should start with a website and gradually work towards gaining resources. Roles of Narsimha and Ashish are also not to be undermined. They helped a lot in building networks, making sure the legal aspects of the project and all in all to make the vision a reality i.e. BOOFLIX.

Behind The Scenes

To keep this website up and running is a tremendous task. In its earlier days when it was not known by much, the three founders were able to manage and maintain the site, but as the audience and demand grew(for more shows) our three founders were having a difficult time maintaining the site. So they started hiring some employees who were of thier same batch and when even that could not handle the traffic this website was recieving, they had to add some startup funding to thier already earned profits and go on for a mass recruitment drive. Since then the company hires skilled individuals every now and then to ensure the proper functioning of the website and content delivery to the users. Now as we are a multi million dollars company we have servers all around the globe due to which the website is running smoothly and efficiently.
